# [Unreal Fest 2023](https://www.unrealengine.com/en-US/events/unreal-fest-2023) kicking off!

Thalhalla is attending watch here for more news as we go.

The big news right off the bat is that the Epic CEO announced changes coming to licensing for non-game developers.  Sounds like the film/tv/automotive/commercial guys will be paying an enterprise license like adobe or maya coming soon.  Game developers will not be affect we were assured, and the royalty of 5% beyond your first $1 million will not increase, if anything it will decrease.  And you can always contact their sales department and negotiate a non-royalties based license.
