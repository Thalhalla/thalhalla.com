## Optimizing UE5

Unreal Engine’s new graphics features offer incredible power, and have changed the game in terms of performance. But everyone knew the rules for the old game, and some of the new rules may seem counterintuitive if you played the old game. In this first of two sessions, we’ll dive into Nanite and Lumen to show you how you can best leverage these features for making visually sophisticated, performant projects.
